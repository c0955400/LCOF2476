Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of trash cans do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of trash cans including kitchen bins, outdoor bins, recycling bins, and smart trash cans.

2. Are your trash cans eco-friendly?

Yes, many of our trash cans are made from recycled materials and designed to support sustainable waste management practices.

3. Do you provide bulk discounts for businesses?

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for commercial and business orders. Please contact our sales team for details.

4. How do I clean and maintain my trash can?

We recommend washing your trash can with warm soapy water and drying it thoroughly. For smart bins, refer to the user manual for specific care instructions.

5. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the product is in its original condition. Please visit our Return Policy page for more details.

6. Do your trash cans come with a warranty?

Yes, all our products come with a one-year warranty covering manufacturing defects.
